Cloud Messaging Platform

A revolutionay cloud based system to easily send SMS messages

Our world class Cloud Messaging Platform is able to deliver 2-way bulk SMS messages over 2G, 3G, and LTE networks across the globe.

Features & Benefits:

  • Provides a cloud-based, one-to-many communication capable of integrating with multiple protocols, for any carrier, enterprise, employee or consumer &endash; anywhere and anytime
  • State of the art web-messaging portal enables users to upload and manage groups and contacts, and send messages to nearly limitless group sizes with ease and convenience
  • Provides integration into existing communication channels such as email, pagers, and telematics for high volume, highly available mass message delivery
  • Supports premium messaging events (e.g. coupon), and embedded promotions for high-volume marketing campaign management
  • Scalable redundant platform allows for operational continuity assurances for enterprises
  • The REST provides developers or enterprises to quickly and easily integrate messaging into the CRM call flow
  • The CMC analytics dashboard automates reports and easily provides messaging statistics
